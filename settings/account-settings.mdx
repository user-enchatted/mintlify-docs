# ⚙️ Account Settings

The **Account Settings** section allows you to manage your personal information and take control of your data.

## What you can update
- **Name**: Update the full name associated with your account
- **Email**: Change the email address used for login and notifications

All changes are saved immediately and take effect across the platform.

<Info title="Privacy by Design">
    All your account data is encrypted and securely stored. Only you have access to your bots and settings.
</Info>

You can access your settings by clicking **Account Settings** from the sidebar on the main dashboard.
