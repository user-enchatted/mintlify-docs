# 🔐 Logging In

To access your Assignbots workspace, log in using your email and password at [app.assignbots.ai](https://app.assignbots.ai/).

---

### 🪪 How to Log In

1. Visit [https://app.assignbots.ai/](https://app.assignbots.ai/)
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**
3. Click **Log In**

![Login Screen](../../images/login-screen.png)

Optional:
- ✅ Use the **Remember me** checkbox to stay logged in on your device

---

### ❓ Forgot Your Password?

Click the **“Forgot your password?”** link below the login form. You’ll be prompted to enter your email address and receive a secure password reset link.

---

### 👤 Don't Have an Account Yet?

You can [create an account](./creating-an-account) in just a few steps.

<Info title="Security Reminder">
    Your login credentials are never shared. All access is encrypted and protected under our strict privacy policy.
</Info>
